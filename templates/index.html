README: MCQA System

System Introduction:

The MCQA System is designed for multiple-choice question answering. It allows users to input a question and four answer options (A/B/C/D) and provides predictions based on the most likely answer according to the model.

Usage Instructions:

  • Enter the question and answers into the input fields provided.
  • Click the "Submit" button to process the input.
  • The prediction results will appear in the "customizedResult" section, and the ranked answers will be shown in the "rankingResult" section.

Example:

  • Who was president of the United States when Bill Clinton was born?
  • A. Herbert Hoover
  • B. Harry S. Truman
  • C. Franklin Roosevelt
  • D. Dwight Eisenhower
The correct answer is B.
